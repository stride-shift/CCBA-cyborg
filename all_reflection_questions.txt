CYBORG HABITS - ALL REFLECTION QUESTIONS

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
EXECUTIVE CHALLENGE SET (15 Days)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Day 1: Strategic Clarity
How clear is your strategy to someone hearing it for the first time? What would make it clearer?

Day 2: Team Performance
What makes performance conversations effective versus awkward? How can you improve yours?

Day 3: Decision Velocity
Where does decision-making slow down in your organization? How can you speed it up appropriately?

Day 4: Stakeholder Messaging
How do you adjust your message for different audiences? What stays the same and what changes?

Day 5: Resource Allocation
Are your resources aligned with your stated priorities? If not, what's driving the disconnect?

Day 6: Future Scenarios
How would your strategy change if you knew which future would happen? Should you change it now?

Day 7: Change Leadership
What makes people resist change? How can you address those concerns before they become blockers?

Day 8: Decision Bias Check
Which biases are you most prone to? How can you build checks into your decision process?

Day 9: Executive Presence
What creates executive presence in communication? How can you strengthen yours?

Day 10: Time & Energy Audit
Is your calendar aligned with your priorities? What's crowding out what matters most?

Day 11: Succession Pipeline
Who is ready to step up when you need them? What are you doing to prepare them?

Day 12: Board Effectiveness
What makes a board meeting effective versus a waste of everyone's time? How can you improve yours?

Day 13: Risk Intelligence
What's the difference between paranoia and preparedness? How do you know which risks to focus on?

Day 14: Innovation Culture
Does your culture enable innovation or crush it? What needs to change, starting with you?

Day 15: Leadership Legacy
What impact do you want to have? Are your daily actions building toward it or away from it?


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STANDARD CHALLENGE SET (15 Days)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Day 1: Clarity Challenge
What surprised you most about the gap between what you thought was clear communication and what actually landed clearly?

Day 2: Culture Rules Challenge
What invisible rule or "that's just how we do things" moment did you uncover, and how long do you think it's been holding things back?

Day 3: Priority Challenge
Tell us about the task you discovered you've been prioritizing that actually keeps you in the same place - what made you realize this?

Day 4: Decision Challenge
What was it like to sit with the choice that scared you versus the one that felt safe? What did you notice about yourself in that moment?

Day 5: Message Challenge
When you stripped your message down to its core, what did you discover you were really trying to say? How different was it from what you started with?

Day 6: Opposite Strategy Challenge
What problems did you realize your current approach might be accidentally creating? What would change if you flipped your perspective?

Day 7: Efficiency Challenge
Share the "essential" task you discovered might not need to exist at all - what was your reaction when you realized this?

Day 8: Bias Detection Challenge
What assumption about how others think were you making that created blind spots you couldn't see? How did this show up in your work?

Day 9: Feedback Challenge
Describe the moment you realized there was a gap between what you meant to communicate and what someone else was actually hearing.

Day 10: Energy Challenge
What did you learn about your natural energy patterns that you'd been working against? How might you design differently going forward?

Day 11: Future Planning Challenge
What connection did you make between your future breakthrough and something small you could do right now? What are you curious to try?

Day 12: Jargon Translation Challenge
Tell us about the "clear" instructions that turned out to be confusing - what did you realize about how you've been approaching complex projects?

Day 13: Failure Analysis Challenge
What part of your plan felt rock-solid until you looked for failure points? What did that reveal about how you typically prepare for launches?

Day 14: Obsolescence Challenge
Describe the routine you realized exists only because you'd never questioned it - what would you experiment with instead?

Day 15: Avoidance Challenge
What did you discover you were really avoiding underneath that task you keep putting off? How did it feel to name the real resistance?


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TEST CHALLENGE SET (5 Days)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Day 1: Test Day 1 - Getting Started
What motivated you to join this program? What do you hope to achieve?

Day 2: Test Day 2 - Building Awareness
What patterns did you notice about your energy levels today?

Day 3: Test Day 3 - Taking Action
How did it feel to complete that task you had been avoiding? What did you learn?

Day 4: Test Day 4 - Connection
What did you notice when you focused completely on listening rather than responding?

Day 5: Test Day 5 - Reflection
What insights have you gained from this first week? What will you do differently next week?
